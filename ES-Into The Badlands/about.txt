Endless Sky: Into the Badlands is alternate start, placing you as captain of the H.M.S Serene, a Navy special forces gunboat. Will you have what it takes to survive being trapped in a nightmare?
**Warning: This start is designed as a challenge, it is HIGHLY RECOMMENDED you are familiar with all game mechanics & solo ship strategies before attempting**

Features:
	-Fully compatable with base game story, taking place 1 year before the normal start point
	-New storyline with 2 unique endings
	-New region: the Badlands
	-2 new factions: The Swarm & The Holdout
	-_ new ships, _ new outfits
